Nainital Mukteshwar Hill Station Tour Packages

Delhi-Nainital-Mukteshwar-Delhi
Duration

03 Nights/04 Days

Tour type

Holidays Tour

Group size

50 people

Location

Delhi-Nainital-Mukteshwar

Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi – Nainital

Meet our Representative at Delhi Airport/ Railway Station and drive to Nainital. On arrival check-in at the hotel. After Fresh N Up Visit the evening at Mall road by yourself, Overnight stay at the hotel.


Day 2: Nainital

After breakfast Visit Naina Devi Temple, an Evening boat ride at glittered Naini Lake (on your own), and enjoy the leisure Overnight stay at the hotel.


Day 3: Nainital - Mukteshwar

After breakfast, we will drive to Mukteshwar. Upon arrival, transfer to the hotel and relax after that visit Chauli Ki Jali, Mukteshwar Dham Temple, Bhalu Gaad falls, and come back to the hotel Overnight.


Day 4: Mukteshwar - Delhi

After breakfast Drive to Delhi and Drop you with sweet memories……
