Mussoorie Jim Corbett Safari Tour

Delhi-Mussoorie-Jim Corbett- Delhi
Duration

04 Nights/05 Days

Tour type

Holidays Tour

Group size

50 people

Location

Delhi-Mussoorie-Jim Corbett

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive Delhi to Mussoorie

Arrive Delhi. Pick up and transfer by surface to Mussoorie. Late afternoon arrive in Mussoorie & check in at your hotel. Evening at leisure. Overnight at the hotel.


Day 2: Mussorie Sightseeing

After breakfast, go to different destinations of Mussorie such as; Kempty falls, Surkanda Devi Temple, Camel Back Road, Mussoorie Fall. One can enjoy the trolly ride to Gun Hill to view the splendid view of all the city and ice-capped Himalaya. Overnight at Mussoorie.


Day 3: Mussoorie – Corbett

Drive to Jim Corbett National Park. Check in upon arrival. You may relax for the rest of the day. Overnight stay at the hotel.


Day 4: Jim Corbett National Park

Spend the day in Corbett. Go on an early morning safari in the park. Go for local sightseeing of Jim Corbett like; Corbett Museum, Girija temple and Jhulapul ( Jhula is abridge made of iron rods with suspension). Overnight at Corbett.


Day 5: Corbett - Delhi

After breakfast Drive to Delhi and Drop you with sweet memories……
