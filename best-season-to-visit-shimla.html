Best Season to Visit Shimla

March to June & October to December is Best Season to Visit Shimla

Shimla often referred to as the "Queen of the Hills", can be visited all year round. However, winter weather and summer from October to June are the best seasons to travel to Shimla. Winters are cool and temperatures can fall below freezing this is the ideal time for travelers who like to enjoy the snowfall in Shimla. This is also the prime time for honeymooners to witness snowstorms, and skiing in Shimla is one of the most interesting winter tours. Sports. Summer in Shimla is warm with high quality and cool nights, attracting a large number of tourists.
Summer is the right season for excursions such as paragliding, trekking, tent camping, and rafting.

December to January & May to June is Peak Season in Shimla

December to January and summer is the peak season in Shimla. Most of the travelers planning to go to Shimla in the New Year period and the resorts usually have high prices sometime during this season. Summer is the busiest season in Shimla to travel with the best weather and at this time everything to do on the trip is packed with long queues. During the summer season, the hotels in Shimla are also fully booked and it is recommended to book at least one month before departure.
